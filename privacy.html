Last Updated: February 6, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use our services, including contact information such as name, email address, and phone number.

2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including to facilitate SMS communication for appointment booking.

3. 2026 SMS Compliance & Data Sharing

Strict Adherence to A2P 10DLC Standards:

  • No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.
  • We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal data to third parties.
  • Data is only shared with service providers (like Twilio) strictly necessary to deliver the SMS service.

4. Opt-In & Opt-Out (Unsubscribe)

By providing your phone number to us (via our website forms or by calling our tracked numbers), you consent to receive automated text messages from us regarding your inquiries and appointments.

To opt-out: Users can text STOP to any message to cancel at any time. You will receive a final confirmation message and no further texts will be sent.
